Interactive Media Week Three

This was my developed homepage before gaining more inspiration, however after more research I had decided to scrap this homepage and focus on a different path in terms of style and functionality

In search of a new direction, I had stumbled on a interactive website called Spellverse and I really liked the style. In this welcomepage I had aimed to mimic that style and bring a similar functionality as the introudction to my webpage.

One class activity we did was to sketch a full interaction of the website, so including all the webpages and what every button on the page leads to, this creates a chart that showcases what interaction is needed where.

In this class activity, we learnt how to import desired fonts through google fonts, how to insert iframes into our webpages and creating a responsive website for mobile.
