Welcome to Retrospket, A work book inspired by the retro technology in the 1970s and 1980s and The 1999 film "The Matrix"

So why Retrospekt? When originally starting the physical computing workshops, It took me back, seeing all the primitive machines and how it functions with code reminded me of retro technology and how the computer is just made of code as well. Hence the I played on the word "retrospect" which is like looking back to the past

As a way to document my physical computing, I had made a 3d model of a circuit we made every week documenting its function and other information along with it, I had also added a reflection for each week.

Take a look and I hope you enjoy Retrospekt - Owen
